# **App Name**: Neup Group

## Core Features:

- Hero Section with Clear Messaging: Presents Neup Group's identity and vision, showcasing what the company is and where it's headed. This will include a compelling headline, a concise one-line statement, and up to two clear calls to action.
- Company Overview (Authority Block): Provides a snapshot of Neup Group's details including its legal name, type of company, and a succinct origin story, highlighting its vision, long-term focus, and execution prowess.
- Venture Portfolio Display: Central to the site, showcasing Neup Group's various ventures like Tourio, Neup Marketing, and others, through logo, name, one-line value proposition, and a status badge indicating their current phase (Live, Building, Beta).
- Capability Showcase: Outlines Neup Group's capabilities categorized into platforms, SaaS products, digital ecosystems, marketing infrastructure, AI applications, and scalable consumer/B2B products, demonstrating a systems-thinking approach.
- Vision and Philosophy Statements: Expresses Neup Group's guiding principles, emphasizing long-term value, product focus, systems, talent development, and a Nepal-first, global-ready mindset.
- Differentiation Explanation: Explains why Neup Group's existence is meaningful, such as building products Nepal lacks, creating opportunities for young talent, connecting digital systems, and scaling ideas into platforms.
- Partnership and Career Section: Provides pathways for partnerships, job opportunities, and internships, attracting early believers, strategic partners, and talent.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: black (#4B0082) to convey vision and trustworthiness.
- Background color: Very light grey (#F0F0F0) to provide a neutral canvas that enhances readability and emphasizes the content.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font, for clear and modern text. Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a geometric sans-serif font, is used for headlines to establish a modern technology-driven look.
- Simple, clean icons that represent ventures and capabilities. To be used sparingly, but thoughtfully.
- Clean, spacious layout with clear section divisions. Dark or neutral theme.
- Subtle transitions on scroll and hover effects for a premium user experience.
